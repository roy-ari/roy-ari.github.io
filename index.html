Research Interests
    I am primarily interested in analytic number theory, algebraic number theory, special functions, and graph theory. More particularly in zeros of the Riemann zeta-function and $L$ -functions, partial sums of zeta and $L$-functions, divisor and circle problems, integral transform, and the zeta functions of graphs.
